# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

B2B and B2C customers in the Berlin area looking for transport and logistics services. Business clients need reliable freight, warehousing, or supply chain support. Private customers need moving services or one-off transports. They arrive at the site to evaluate trustworthiness and make contact.

## Product Purpose

Klee Transport & Logistik is a sole proprietorship (Einzelunternehmen) offering transport and logistics services in and around Berlin. The website establishes credibility, communicates services, and drives inquiries via email and phone. Currently a Coming Soon page while the full site is built.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, strong, grounded. The brand conveys trust and security — a company that delivers what it promises. Tone is direct and confident, not flashy. The green-and-dark color scheme ties to the road/movement motif in the logo.

## Anti-references

- Overly flashy or animated logistics sites that feel like tech startups
- Cheap template sites with stock photos of generic trucks
- Overly corporate/cold enterprise logistics portals

## Design Principles

1. **Trust at first glance** — Every element should reinforce reliability. No gimmicks.
2. **Show strength quietly** — Bold presence without being loud. Confidence, not aggression.
3. **Direct path to contact** — The site exists to generate inquiries. Remove friction.
4. **Grounded in reality** — Real business, real address, real person. Authenticity over polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast on dark backgrounds. Touch targets sized for mobile (drivers checking on phone). German-language primary.
